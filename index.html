Hi!

The Michael J. Petrides School is a public school located at 715 Ocean Terrace in Staten Island, New York. It was created by Board of Education officials and named for their late colleague and College of Staten Island professor, Michael J. Petrides (1941â€“1994). The school opened in 1995, on the former campus of the college. Students apply to attend the school through a lottery system. However, 8th graders going into high school, who score in the top 2% on the ELA 7th grade test, can receive auto-admission if Petrides is the first choice on their application. The current principal is Joanne Buckheit. Petrides educates students from Kindergarten through 12th, or senior year in high school. It has an assistant principal for each grade category (early childhood, elementary, middle school, high school)..
